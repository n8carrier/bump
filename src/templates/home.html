Gain Control of Your Restaurant's Queue



Text/Email Notifications for Guests

Do you enjoy a half hour wait in the lobby for your table? Neither do your guests. They also don't like being constrained to 100 feet by your pager system. With Bump you can allow your guests to sign up for text or email notifications when their table is ready. That way they can do what they want while they wait.





Close the Loop

Looking for insight into when your guests will actually be ready to be seated? Guess no longer. Bump gives you the power to send notifications to your guests and let's them respond if they're running late. That gives you the power to keep your tables full and your guests happy.





Gain Marketing Insights

Bump allows you to gain insight into the frequency of visits of your loyal customers. Have you noticed some of your main customers have been coming less frequently? Send them coupons and promos to keep them coming. As long as your guests have checked the box to allow you to send coupons and promos, you have the power to leverage targeted advertising and keep your business running well.
