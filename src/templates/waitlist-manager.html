Live Free with Bump Waitlist Manager



Manage your Waitlist, Completely Free

Digitize your waitlist at no cost. Bump allows you to keep track of all parties on your waitlist, including the name, party size, arrival time, wait estimate, and target seating time. Don't risk mistakes, start using Bump for free today.

  • Let your guests know when their table is ready, by SMS or email
  • Add guests directly from the Bump waitlist manager, or allow guests to sign-in with a tablet (see below)
  • Allow guests to reply when they need more time, enabling you to know when your tables will be filled
  • Preserve valuable data that can help you run your business, such as average wait times, accuracy in predicting seating time, and trends in party size.




Give your Guests Freedom

Do you enjoy a half hour wait in the lobby for your table? Neither do your guests. They also don't like being constrained to 100 feet by restaurant pager systems. With Bump, you can allow your guests to sign up for text or email notifications when their table is ready. That way they can do what they want while they wait.

  • Provide your guests with the flexibility they desire
  • Allow guests to sign up for SMS or Email notifications through a tablet interface
  • Offer your guests the opportunity to sign up for coupons and promos
  • Eliminate the need to invest in expensive pager systems




Gain Marketing Insights

Bump allows you to gain insight into the frequency of visits of your loyal customers. Have you noticed some of your main customers have been coming less frequently? Send them coupons and promos to keep them coming. As long as your guests have checked the box to allow you to send coupons and promos, you have the power to leverage targeted advertising and keep your business running well.

  • Understand how often your guests come and when they drop off
  • Leverage targeted messaging on the preferred channel of your customer
  • Analyze trends to better manage your restaurant
